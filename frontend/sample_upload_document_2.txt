Data Analyst Internship Opportunity

Organization: Insight Metrics Lab
Location: Boston, MA (Hybrid)
Duration: Summer Internship

About the Role
Insight Metrics Lab is seeking a data analyst intern to work on analytics dashboards and reporting automation.
The role focuses on data quality checks, exploratory analysis, and communicating findings to stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities
- Clean and transform raw datasets for reporting.
- Build SQL queries for recurring business questions.
- Create visual dashboards and summary reports.
- Identify trends, anomalies, and potential risks in data.
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams on metric definitions.

Required Qualifications
- Strong SQL and spreadsheet skills.
- Basic Python for data manipulation.
- Familiarity with data visualization concepts.
- Clear written communication and presentation skills.

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with business intelligence tools.
- Exposure to cloud data platforms and ETL workflows.
- Interest in product analytics and experimentation.

Success Criteria
At the end of the internship, the candidate should deliver:
1. A reusable reporting workflow.
2. A dashboard tracking key operational metrics.
3. A written summary of trends and recommendations.

Working Style
The team values curiosity, fast iteration, and evidence-based decision making.
